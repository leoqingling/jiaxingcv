★ Best Live Demo, ISCAS 2026

Semi-Static Magnetic Field Localization for WPT

A 6.78 MHz flexible transmitter-coil array and Monte Carlo localization algorithm for real-time 5-DoF receiver localization in wireless power transfer.

Interactive demonstrations included a whack-a-mole game and an International Space Station docking simulation.

Mean localization error: 6.75 mm; received power: 2.92 mW at 72 mm.
